Frequently Asked Questions

Are DTCP-approved plots available in Guntakal?

Yes, MyPlotMitra lists DTCP-approved residential plots in Guntakal from verified owners. Look for the “Verified Property” badge on a listing, and always ask the seller for approval documents before paying any advance.

What is the typical plot size for sale in Guntakal?

Plot sizes in Guntakal commonly range from 30x40 ft (1,200 sq ft) to larger sizes of 40x60 ft (2,400 sq ft) and above, depending on the layout and location within the town.

Is registration and stamp duty included in the plot price?

No. The price shown on MyPlotMitra listings is generally the property price only. Registration charges and stamp duty in Andhra Pradesh are paid separately at the Sub-Registrar's office and vary by transaction value.
