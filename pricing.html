Pricing & How MyPlotMitra Makes Money

A plain-language summary of our business model, for buyers, sellers, and our payment partners.
Line of Business

MyPlotMitra is an online property classifieds and listing platform serving Anantapur, Hyderabad, and surrounding areas in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. We connect property owners directly with buyers. We are not a real estate broker, agent, or transaction platform — we do not buy, sell, hold, or take commission on any property, and no property sale value ever passes through our payment account. We monetize only through small, fixed-price digital service fees described below.

How the payment flow works

  • A user browses listings for free — no payment required to view, search, or contact via chat.
  • A user optionally chooses a paid feature (a badge, a boost, or contact credits) from a fixed price list.
  • Payment is collected once, directly by MyPlotMitra, for that specific digital service.
  • We do not collect, hold, or forward any part of a property's sale price. Property price negotiation and payment (if any) happens entirely off-platform, directly between the buyer and seller — MyPlotMitra is not a party to that transaction.
What this is not: We are not an escrow service, not a real estate brokerage, and we do not process real estate transaction values. Average payment on our platform is under ₹500.
