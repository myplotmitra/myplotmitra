CREATE YOUR ICONS HERE
======================
Go to canva.com and create a 512×512 image:
  - Background color: #E85D04 (orange)
  - Text: "1%" in white, Playfair Display Bold, large font
  - Download as PNG named icon-512.png

Then go to: https://pwa-image-generator.vercel.app
  - Upload your icon-512.png
  - Download all sizes
  - Place them here:
      icon-72.png
      icon-96.png
      icon-128.png
      icon-192.png
      icon-512.png
